About Us

Welcome to Bhaarat ki Rasoi – A Culinary Journey Through India

At Bharat ki Rasoi, we are passionate about bringing the authentic flavors of India to your table. Our restaurant was founded on the belief that every dish has a story to tell, and we aim to share these stories with you through each bite. From the bustling streets of Mumbai to the serene valleys of Kashmir, our menu is a carefully curated collection of India's diverse culinary heritage.

Pani Puri

From Kolkata to Mumbai Savor the explosive burst of flavors with Pani Puri, enjoyed from the lanes of Kolkata to the beaches of Mumbai. These bite-sized hollow puris filled with tamarind water, chili, chaat masala, potato, onion, and chickpeas are the quintessence of Indian street food.

Price: ₹ 220
